PROJECTS

EUREKA COWORKING

INTERIOR DESIGN
PROJECT STATUS: COMPLETED in 2021

Post Covid situation demanded the work from home scenario, creating rising requirement for Coworking spaces in every city. The project "Eureka Coworking" creates a vibrant environment for working and yet values the importance of creative working by providing both indoor & outdoor breakout spaces in balcony, Terraces, pasaages etc. The warm colours and simple geometries used elevates the mood alongwith adjusting to everyone's choice of their personal space.
